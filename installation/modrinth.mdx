Installing Flare through Modrinth is the simplest and most convenient way to get started. Modrinth automatically handles downloading, installing, and updating mods for you. 

## Before You Begin

1. **Download and Install Modrinth:** If you haven't already, download the Modrinth launcher from the official website: [https://modrinth.com/](https://modrinth.com/) and follow the installation instructions.
2. **Ensure Java is Installed:** Minecraft and its mods require Java to run. Download the latest version from: [https://www.java.com/download/](https://www.java.com/download/).

## Installation Steps

1. **Open the Modrinth Launcher:** Log in to your Modrinth account or create one if you don't have one. 
2. **Search for Flare:** In the Modrinth launcher, use the search bar to find the "Flare" modpack.
3. **Select the Modpack:** Click on the Flare modpack from the search results to view its page.
4. **Install the Modpack:** Click on the "Install" button. Modrinth will automatically handle downloading all necessary mods and creating a new Minecraft instance for Flare.
5. **Launch and Play:** Once the installation is complete, you'll find Flare listed in your Modrinth launcher's list of profiles. Click "Play" to launch the modpack!

## Troubleshooting

* **Slow Download:** If the download seems slow, check your internet connection and try pausing/resuming the download.
* **Installation Issues:** If you encounter any errors, try restarting the Modrinth launcher or your computer.

If problems persist, check the [Frequently Asked Questions](/docs/faq) or reach out for support on our [Discord Server](link-to-your-discord). 

Happy gaming!
